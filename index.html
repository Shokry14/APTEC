About Us

We are a leading Egyptain company in the construction, maintenance and dismantling of renewable facilities,wind and photovoltaic . Our great values ​are our extensive experience with more than 16 years in the renewable sector, innovation and the rigorous specialization of our human capital


This has allowed us to respond to complex jobs that, due to their difficulty and urgency, cannot be assumed by others. Added to this differential value is a fast , proactive , efficient and safe service that manages to reduce costs and downtime, improving the efficiency of the facilities. Thanks to this unique way of working, Altertec Renovables has garnered great national and international prestige among the world's major energy companies.


Our greatest challenge is to be a world leader in the maintenance of wind and photovoltaic installations.
